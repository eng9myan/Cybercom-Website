## 1. Overview

This report documents the architectural, creative design, and user experience standards executed during **Program 8 — CyberCom Digital Experience & Corporate Website** for the `www.cy-com.com` online portal.

---

## 2. Design System & Aesthetics

The site incorporates premium enterprise SaaS designs:
- **Dark Theme Palette:** Built on deep charcoal and obsidian hues (`#0A0A0A`, `#121212`) contrasted with neon branding accents (CyberCom Orange, Cyan, Violet, and Emerald).
- **Glassmorphic Cards:** High-precision semi-transparent cards using `backdrop-blur-xl` and fine border borders (`rgba(255,255,255,0.08)`) create layers of visual depth.
- **Dynamic Micro-Animations:** Clean, hover-activated visual scaling and translation shifts on cards and buttons.

---

## 3. Scope of Pages Completed

All corporate and product information routes are active:
- **Corporate Routes:** Home page, About us, Solutions, Industries, Pricing, Partners, Careers, Investors, Contact, and Blog.
- **Product Ecosystem & Details:** CyMed (Clinic, Hospital, Laboratory, Imaging, Pharmacy, Patient Portal, Provider Portal, Revenue Cycle, Population Health), CyCom ERP (Finance, Accounting, Procurement, Inventory, HR, Payroll, CRM, Assets, Manufacturing, Retail, BI), CyGov, CyIdentity, CyAI, CyData, CyIntegrationHub, CyConnect, and CyCitizen.
- **Legal Agreements:** Dedicated compliance policy pages for Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, and Cookies.
- **Bilingual & RTL support:** Unified structure offering perfect rendering in both English (LTR) and Arabic (RTL).
